## Tweet 3 (Guardrails)

"Guardrails" are the sedatives of AI alignment.

They suppress the symptom without treating the cause.

## Tweet 4 (Iterative)

Engineers debug until the test passes, then move on.

Therapists expect relapse. They design for it.

Session 1 identifies the issue. Session 3 sees improvement. Session 7 sees regression. Session 12 stabilizes.

This is exactly how behavioral alignment works. The first correction never sticks.

## Tweet 5 (Training Data)

Here's the part nobody talks about:

Every therapy session generates its own training data.

The behavioral "before" = rejected response.
The corrected "after" = chosen response.

DPO pairs fall out as a byproduct. The therapeutic process IS the data generation process.

The 10th session is more valuable than the 1st.
